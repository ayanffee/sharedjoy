Questions

Good to know

Does the person I send it to need an account or app?

No. They just tap your link and it plays in their browser on any phone or computer. Accounts are only for creators who want to publish and track replies.

Can I use it for a wedding or event with lots of guests?

Yes — that's a favourite use. Add a question (dropdown, short answer or vote) to a screen and guests must answer to continue, so you collect RSVPs and see every reply in your dashboard.

What's free vs Premium?

You can build and send real letters for free. Premium unlocks videos, all themes, longer messages, unlimited extra screens, and viewing replies/responses.

How does billing work?

Premium is a subscription — choose monthly or annual (annual saves 20%). It renews automatically and you can cancel anytime. Payments are handled securely by Paystack.

Do my photos and videos really travel in the link?

Yes. Everything you add is saved and delivered with the letter, so recipients see your media exactly as you arranged it.
